Privacy Policy 

This Privacy Policy explains how Random Games collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website. By accessing or using Random Games, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Random Games is a browser-based gaming platform where users can play HTML5 games online without downloading or installing any software.

Information We Collect

Random Games does not require users to create accounts or provide personal information to play games. However, like most websites, we may automatically collect basic technical data such as:

  • Browser type
  • Device type
  • Operating system
  • IP address (for security and traffic analysis)
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site

This information is used only to understand how the website is used and to improve performance and stability.

Cookies

Random Games may use cookies to improve user experience and measure website traffic. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us understand how users interact with the website.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some parts of the website may not function correctly without them.

How We Use Information

The information we collect is used only for:

  • Improving website performance
  • Monitoring traffic and usage
  • Fixing bugs and technical issues
  • Keeping the platform secure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

No Downloads or Installations

All games on Random Games run directly in your browser using HTML5. We do not require you to download or install any files, apps, or plugins. This helps keep your device safe and reduces security risks.

Third-Party Content

Some games available on Random Games are provided by third-party developers. These games run within the browser and do not have access to your personal files or private data.

Random Games is not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites that may be linked from within games.

Children’s Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Random Games complies with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect, store, or request personal information from children under the age of 13.

Since Random Games does not require user accounts or personal details, children can play games without sharing private information. If a parent or guardian believes that personal data has been shared, they can contact us and we will remove it promptly.

Data Security

We use standard security measures to protect our website and users. Because no sensitive personal data or accounts are stored, the risk to users is very low.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and continued use of Random Games means you accept the updated policy.
