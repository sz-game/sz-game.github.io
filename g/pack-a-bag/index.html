How to Play Pack a Bag Unblocked

The gameplay of Pack a Bag Unblocked revolves around selecting items and placing them carefully inside a bag. Each level presents a set of objects that must all fit into the given space. As levels progress, item shapes become more complex, increasing the difficulty naturally.
