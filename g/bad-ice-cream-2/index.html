Strategy and Pro Tips

A key strategy in Bad Ice Cream 2 is learning when to create ice and when to destroy it. Blocking an enemy too early can trap you, while breaking ice at the wrong moment can open paths for danger. Always leave yourself an escape route before committing to a move.
