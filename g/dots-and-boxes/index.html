How to Play Dots and Boxes Unblocked

The objective of Dots and Boxes Unblocked is to complete more boxes than your opponent by drawing lines between dots. Players take turns adding a single line per move, either horizontally or vertically between two adjacent dots. When a player completes the fourth side of a square, that box is claimed and the player earns another turn. The match continues until all possible lines have been drawn and every box on the grid has been claimed. Winning depends on careful planning, controlling the board, and forcing the opponent into making disadvantageous moves.
