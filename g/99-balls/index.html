How to Play 99 Balls Unblocked

The gameplay starts with a single ball and a row of numbered blocks descending slowly from the top. Your task is to aim and shoot balls at the blocks to reduce their numbers to zero. When a block reaches zero, it disappears, opening space for future shots. Each turn ends once all balls have returned to the bottom. After every round, blocks move closer, increasing pressure and forcing better decision-making. The game ends if any block reaches the bottom line, making shot placement critical from the first move to the last.
