How to Play Pudding Monsters Unblocked

The core objective is to merge all pudding monsters into one by swiping them in straight lines. When a pudding moves, it continues sliding until it hits an obstacle or another pudding. This rule means every action has long-term consequences, so careless moves can make a level impossible to finish. Players must analyze the entire board before starting, visualizing how each pudding will travel. Pudding Monsters Unblocked encourages restarting levels and trying alternative paths, reinforcing learning through trial and error.
