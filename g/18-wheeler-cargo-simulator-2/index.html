Strategy and Pro Tips

Driving slowly is often the key to success. Accelerating too quickly can cause loss of control, especially when carrying heavy cargo. Always reduce speed before turning and use the handbrake only when necessary to avoid skidding. Pay attention to the camera angle and switch views if needed to better judge distances. Practicing smooth steering instead of sharp movements will help maintain balance and prevent cargo damage. Restarting a level to learn the route can also improve overall performance.
