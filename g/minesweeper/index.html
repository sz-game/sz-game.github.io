How to Play Minesweeper Unblocked

To play Minesweeper Unblocked, you start with a grid of covered cells. Your goal is to reveal all safe cells without clicking on a mine. When you reveal a cell, it may show a number that tells you how many mines are in the surrounding cells. Using this information, you decide which cells are safe to open next and where mines are likely hidden. Flags help you mark suspected mine locations, allowing you to manage risk and plan your moves more effectively as the grid becomes more complex.

Strategy and Pro Tips

Success in Minesweeper Unblocked depends on careful observation and logical deduction. Start by opening corners or edges, as they often provide safer initial information. Pay close attention to number patterns, especially common formations that indicate guaranteed mine positions. Use flags wisely but avoid over-flagging without certainty, as this can limit your options. When faced with uncertainty, choose moves that minimize risk by opening cells with the highest probability of being safe.
