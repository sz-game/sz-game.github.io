What Makes Raft Wars 2 Different

Unlike many shooting games, progression is tied to upgrading weapons and equipment between battles, which adds a layer of strategy. How to Play Raft Wars 2 Unblocked

To play Raft Wars 2 Unblocked, players take turns aiming and firing projectiles at enemy rafts while floating on water. The goal is to knock opponents into the water or destroy their rafts before they eliminate yours. Each level rewards coins that can be used to upgrade weapons, rafts, and defenses. Players must carefully adjust shot power and angle to account for distance and obstacles. As levels progress, enemies become stronger and smarter, requiring improved upgrades and more precise shots.
