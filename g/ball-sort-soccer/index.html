How to Play Ball Sort Soccer Unblocked

The goal is to sort balls so that each tube contains balls of only one color. You begin with a mixed arrangement across several tubes, some of which may be empty to help with maneuvering. Select a tube to pick up the top ball, then choose another tube to place it—provided the move follows the rules. A ball can only be placed on top of another ball of the same color or into an empty tube. As levels advance, the number of colors and tubes increases, requiring more strategic planning to avoid dead ends.
