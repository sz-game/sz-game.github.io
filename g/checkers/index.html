How to Play Checkers Unblocked

In Checkers Unblocked, the goal is to capture all of your opponent’s pieces or block them so no legal moves remain. Players move diagonally across dark squares, capturing by jumping over opponent pieces. Multiple captures can occur in a single turn if positioning allows. Reaching the far side of the board promotes a piece to a king, granting the ability to move both forward and backward. Matches are turn-based, encouraging careful observation and long-term planning.

Strategy and Pro Tips

A strong Checkers strategy starts with controlling the center of the board, which provides more movement options. Avoid sacrificing pieces early unless it leads to a clear advantage. Always think a few moves ahead, especially when setting up forced captures. Protect your back row as long as possible to prevent the opponent from gaining kings too easily. When you earn a king, use its mobility wisely to apply pressure and control multiple lanes.
