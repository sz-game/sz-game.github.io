How to Play 2048 Cupcakes Unblocked

The gameplay of 2048 Cupcakes Unblocked revolves around sliding tiles on a square grid. When two identical cupcakes collide, they merge into a higher-level cupcake. Every move causes a new cupcake to appear, so planning ahead is essential. The game ends when no more valid moves are available. Players must think carefully about positioning, creating space, and setting up future merges. While the rules are easy to understand, mastering the game requires patience and forward thinking.
