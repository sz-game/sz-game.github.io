To play Level Devil Unblocked, the objective is to guide the character to the exit door at the end of each level. While the controls are simple, the environment is not. Progress in Level Devil Unblocked depends on patience. Taking time to observe platforms and testing movements carefully can help avoid common mistakes. Since restarting a level is instant, experimenting with different approaches is an important part of the gameplay loop.
