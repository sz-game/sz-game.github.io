What Makes Pac Man World Different

Pac Man World stands out by transforming a maze-based arcade legend into a platform-focused experience. The inclusion of jumping, hovering, and charged attacks adds layers of strategy that are not present in classic Pac-Man titles.

Another key difference is the variety of objectives. Levels are not only about avoiding ghosts but also about collecting items, defeating enemies, and completing challenges that require timing and precision. Boss fights further separate Pac Man World from traditional arcade gameplay, giving players memorable encounters that test both movement and combat skills.
