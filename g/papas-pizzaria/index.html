How to Play Papas Pizzaria Unblocked

The game begins with you stepping into the role of a pizza chef responsible for the entire restaurant workflow. You start by taking customer orders and carefully noting their topping choices and baking preferences. After preparing the pizza dough, you add toppings according to the order, bake the pizza for the correct duration, slice it properly, and serve it. Performance is graded after each order, influencing tips and unlocking upgrades that improve efficiency and customer satisfaction.
