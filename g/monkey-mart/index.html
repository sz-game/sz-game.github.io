How to Play Monkey Mart Unblocked

To play Monkey Mart Unblocked, you start with a small shop and a single product. You move your character to plant bananas, harvest them, and place them on shelves for customers. As you earn money, you can unlock new products, machines, and store sections. Hiring assistant monkeys helps automate tasks, allowing you to focus on expansion and upgrades. The game continues endlessly, letting you grow your mart at your own pace.
