Strategy and Pro Tips

Staying near the center of platforms often gives you more reaction time when obstacles start moving. Avoid jumping unnecessarily, as jumping at the wrong moment can throw off your balance. Watching other players can help you anticipate which areas of the map will become unsafe next. Diving is useful for recovering after a slip, but overusing it can make you lose control. Most importantly, remain calm during the final moments, as panicking usually leads to mistakes.
