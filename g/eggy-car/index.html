How to Play Eggy Car Unblocked

To play Eggy Car Unblocked, you guide a small car carrying an egg across an endless series of hills. The goal is to travel as far as possible without dropping the egg. Accelerating too fast can cause the egg to bounce out, while braking at the wrong moment can tip it forward. The game encourages smooth movements and rhythm, teaching players to read the terrain ahead and adjust speed gradually for maximum distance.
