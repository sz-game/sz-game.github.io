How to Play Deal or No Deal Unblocked

The gameplay begins with selecting a single case that becomes yours for the entire session. After that, you open other cases one by one, revealing their hidden values. At set intervals, the banker presents an offer based on the remaining unopened cases. You must then decide whether to accept the deal or continue playing. The goal is simple: finish with the highest possible amount, either by taking the right deal or by holding onto a valuable case until the end.
