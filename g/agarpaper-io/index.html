Strategy and Pro Tips

One of the best strategies in Agarpaper Io Unblocked is to stay near the edges of the map during early gameplay. This reduces the chance of being surrounded by larger players and allows safer growth. As you gain size, gradually move toward more populated areas to find opportunities.

Avoid splitting too early. While splitting can help you capture smaller opponents, it also halves your size and makes you an easy target. Use splits only when you are confident of securing growth or escaping danger. Watching other players’ movement patterns can also help you predict attacks and survive longer.
