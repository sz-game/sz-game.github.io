Chicken Merge Unblocked

How to Play Chicken Merge Unblocked

To play Chicken Merge Unblocked, you begin with basic chickens placed on the game board. By selecting and moving chickens, you can place two identical chickens together to merge them into a higher-level version. As you continue merging, your chickens generate coins that can be collected and used to buy more chickens. The loop is simple: buy, merge, and upgrade. Chicken Merge rewards consistency, so even short sessions help you make progress.

Controls and Key Bindings

PC / Laptop
Mouse Left Click = Select a chicken
Mouse Left Click + Drag = Move a chicken
Mouse Left Click + Drag (onto same level chicken) = Merge chickens
Mouse Left Click (UI buttons) = Buy chickens / Open menus / Collect rewards

Mobile / Tablet
Tap = Select chicken
Tap and Drag = Move chicken
Drag one chicken onto another same level chicken = Merge chickens
Tap (Shop Button) = Buy chickens
Tap (Reward Button) = Collect coins or bonuses
