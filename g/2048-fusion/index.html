How to Play 2048 Fusion Unblocked

The objective is to slide numbered tiles across the grid so that matching values combine into a single, higher-numbered tile. Each move shifts all tiles in one direction, and a new tile appears after every action. Success depends on maintaining open spaces while guiding high-value tiles toward a consistent corner or edge. The game ends when no valid moves remain, encouraging players to think ahead and avoid filling the board too quickly.
