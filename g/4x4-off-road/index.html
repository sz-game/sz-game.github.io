Strategy and Pro Tips

One effective strategy in 4x4 Off Road is to avoid constant full acceleration. Gradual speed control helps maintain balance and prevents flipping on uneven ground. Using the brake lightly before steep hills can make climbs smoother and more predictable.

Another tip is to use steering inputs carefully. Sharp turns at high speed often lead to rollovers. Slower, smoother steering keeps the vehicle stable. Finally, practicing the same track multiple times helps you learn where to slow down and where you can safely accelerate.
