To survive longer in Poor Bunny, focus first on positioning rather than scoring. Staying near the center of the arena gives you more escape options when traps appear. Watch the timing of hazards and learn their patterns so you can predict safe spots before moving. Do not rush for every carrot, especially when multiple traps are active at once.
