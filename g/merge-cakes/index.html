How to Play Merge Cakes Unblocked

Playing Merge Cakes Unblocked is straightforward and intuitive. You start with a small baking counter and a few basic cakes. By placing identical cakes next to each other, you can merge them into a higher-tier cake. Each new cake type generates more coins, helping you expand faster.
