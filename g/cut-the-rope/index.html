Cut the Rope Unblocked

How to Play Cut the Rope Unblocked

To play Cut the Rope Unblocked, you start each level with candy suspended by one or more ropes. Your task is to cut these ropes in the correct sequence so the candy swings, falls, or floats toward Om Nom. Timing is important, as cutting too early or too late can cause the candy to miss its target. Some levels include bubbles that lift the candy or air cushions that push it sideways. Collecting all stars before feeding Om Nom adds an extra layer of challenge. The level is completed only when the candy safely reaches Om Nom’s mouth.
