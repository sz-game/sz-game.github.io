What Are Arcade Games?

Arcade games are fast-paced, skill-driven games designed around short play sessions and repeat attempts. The goal is usually to survive longer, score higher, or clear levels using precise timing and quick reactions. Most arcade games are easy to learn but hard to master, which is why they stay fun even after dozens of plays.
