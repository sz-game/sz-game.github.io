What Are Platformer Games?

Platformer games are action games where the main objective is to move a character through levels by jumping between platforms, avoiding obstacles, and reaching an endpoint. Most levels are designed around timing, spacing, and momentum rather than complex controls.

These games are usually browser-based and easy to understand, but they still demand focus and accuracy. That balance is what keeps platformer games popular across all age groups.
