What Are Simulator Games?

Simulator games are designed to replicate real-world tasks, jobs, or life systems as accurately as possible. Instead of abstract rules, these games use realistic mechanics, time management, and cause-and-effect gameplay.
They often focus on driving, life choices, business operations, or routine activities. The goal is not speed but understanding systems and making decisions that affect outcomes.
