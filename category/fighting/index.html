What Are Fighting Games?

Fighting games are combat-focused games where players control characters in one-on-one or small-group battles. The core gameplay revolves around attacks, blocks, dodges, positioning, and reaction speed. Some games lean toward realism, others toward physics-based chaos, but the goal is always the same: defeat your opponent.
