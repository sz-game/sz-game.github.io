What Are Horror Games?

Horror games are browser-based games built around suspense, fear, and survival. They usually involve escaping enemies, solving simple objectives, or staying alive in hostile environments.

Most horror games rely on atmosphere rather than graphics-heavy systems, which makes them ideal for online play. On Random Games, horror titles are lightweight, fast-loading, and playable instantly in modern browsers.
