What Are Multiplayer Games?

Multiplayer games are online games where two or more players play together at the same time. Instead of playing alone, you compete or cooperate with other real players through your browser.

These games are usually fast-paced, skill-based, and designed for replayability. Most multiplayer games on Random Games use HTML5 technology, which means they load quickly and work smoothly without downloads.
