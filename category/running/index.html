What Are Running Games?

Running games are endless or level-based games where your character automatically moves forward. Your job is to avoid obstacles, time your jumps or turns, and keep going without crashing.

Most running games focus on reaction speed and pattern recognition rather than complex mechanics. They’re designed for short sessions but easy replay, making them perfect for quick breaks.
