What Are Puzzle Games?

Puzzle games focus on solving challenges using logic, numbers, shapes, timing, or spatial thinking. The goal is usually to complete levels by figuring out the correct solution rather than relying on speed or action mechanics.

These games reward patience, planning, and creativity, which is why they’re popular with both casual players and serious problem-solvers.
