Game Overview – What is Granny?

Granny is a thrilling horror survival game where you wake up trapped inside a dark house and must escape within five days. You are locked in Granny's house, and she hears everything you do, so you must move quietly and hide carefully. The game challenges your stealth and puzzle-solving skills as you search for keys, tools, and clues to unlock the main door. Gameplay

Granny gameplay puts you in a tense survival situation where every sound matters. You start in a bedroom and have five days to escape before it's game over. As you explore the creaky house, you must find items like keys, hammers, and weapons hidden in drawers, closets, and secret rooms. Granny patrols the house and rushes toward any noise you make — whether it's dropping objects, stepping on creaky floors, or opening squeaky doors. You can hide under beds, inside wardrobes, or in the bathroom to avoid her. The game becomes more challenging as you learn complex escape routes that require multiple items and careful timing. Each failed escape costs you one day, and Granny gets faster and smarter with each attempt.

How to Play

Press Play to wake up trapped in Granny's house. Move quietly around the house to avoid making noise. Search drawers, shelves, and rooms for keys and tools. Hide under beds or inside closets when you hear Granny coming. Collect items needed to unlock the main door or escape routes. Avoid stepping on bear traps and triggering alarms. Use weapons carefully to temporarily knock Granny down. Escape through the main door before your five days run out.

Granny FAQs

Q1: How do you escape in Granny?

A: In Granny, you can escape in three ways: unlock the front door by disabling its locks, fix and drive the car from the garage, or use the sewer route under the house. Each escape path requires finding and combining multiple items while avoiding Granny.

Q2: How does the 5-day system work in Granny?

A: You have five days (lives) to escape. If Granny catches you, that day ends and you respawn in the starting room with progress retained. Failing all five days leads to a game-over closeout.

Q3: What are the basic controls and interactions in Granny?

A: On PC, WASD controls movement, E is for interaction, Space drops items, and C toggles crouch. You must move carefully, avoid loud actions, and use hiding spots like wardrobes and beds.

Q4: How does stealth and noise affect gameplay in Granny?

A: Granny is highly sound-sensitive: dropping items, walking on creaky floors, or slamming doors can alert her to your location. You’ll need to plan distractions, drop items on soft surfaces, and time your movements carefully.

Q5: What is Practice Mode in Granny?

A: Practice Mode removes Granny’s pursuit, letting you freely explore the house to learn layouts, item locations, and mechanics without the pressure of being chased.

Q6: What key items should you look for in Granny?

A: Look for keys (padlock key, master key), tools (screwdriver, pliers), weapon parts (crossbow, shotgun), and engine parts to repair the car or unlock doors. Each plays a key role in escaping successfully.
