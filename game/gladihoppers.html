Gladihoppers is a hilarious and unique game that mashes up the ancient Roman gladiator battles with crazy physics-based gameplay. You create your own quirky gladiator character and arm them with goofy weapons like rubber chickens and mallets.

The goal is to battle opponents (whether computer-controlled or real players online) in an over-the-top, slapstick fighting arena. But here's the twist - the game uses ragdoll physics, so the fights quickly turn into a riot of flailing limbs and hysterical, unpredictable motion.

How to Play?

The controls in Gladihoppers are simple to pick up. You'll easily learn how to swing weapons, jump around, and roll your gladiator every which way. The real challenge is trying to strategize attacks and moves while your character's body bends and flops in wacky ways due to the ragdoll physics system.

For solo play, there's a campaign mode where you fight through different arenas. For multiplayer madness, you can battle friends or random players online in real-time physics-based combat.

Features:

  • Customizable, silly gladiator characters
  • Harming pixel art visual style
  • Unconventional weapons
